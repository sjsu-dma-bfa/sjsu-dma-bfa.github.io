Artist Statement

I decided on creating my own original character illustrations and models in order to test my own concept creation and originality. What had inspired this project was my interest in anime, video games and VTubers. The central focus for this project in particular are 2d character illustrations alongside 3d modeled characters, in a Pokemon theme. These characters come with their own 3d Pokémon models as well, which were designed by my friend Mary Dominguez. These works fit into the overall flow of my development as an artist, because I aim to go into video game design, specifically in character creation. The central idea of this project is to portray these 3d models alongside their 2d illustrations, and have the 3d models mimic the same movements I make in real time, through face rigging software. The viewers will experience how these models can become more than just regular, still avatars.

Artist Bio

Deana Desilva is a Asian American artist working in digital media art with a focus in character design in 2D illustration and 3D models. She is currently working towards obtaining her Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) degree in Digital Media Art (DMA) at San José State University. Her digital work can be seen on various Twitch streams; such as emotes and VTuber models being used for commercial use. Her focus in digital media art is due to her interest in anime, VTubers and video games. Working in a digital space, she is able to create her own depictions of anime illustrations and 3d character models. Her main inspirations are artists such as y_k_sme, MHKchen, and ddmavis.
