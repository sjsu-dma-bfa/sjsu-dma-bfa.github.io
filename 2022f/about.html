The CADRE Media Lab is proud to present Resonance, an exhibition with both physical and digital artworks available to view in person! Resonance features a wide range of work that manages to expand across a great variety of mediums within digital media art. This final exhibition serves as the platform in which one can experience the culmination of the graduating Seniors’ work in the BFA program.

Resonance reflects on the experience of the artist during polarizing times. In a world permeated by issues surrounding identity, representation, mental health, and embracing cultures past, present, and future, it can feel overwhelming at times. With these issues in mind, the artists create works tackling such topics through reflecting on aspects of the past, present, and future. Through experiencing the art of Resonance, one can reach a greater understanding and gain new perspectives on these topics through exploring this curation of works.
