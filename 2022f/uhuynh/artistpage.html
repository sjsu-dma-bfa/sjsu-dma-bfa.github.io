ARTIST STATEMENT

As an emerging artist, I enjoy working with diverse digital media ranging from video editing, motion graphics, coding, and three-dimensional modeling. I desire to take photos and videos and edit them to document my life. I also enjoy creating personal works for those who are close to me. My work often draws upon a minimalist and cartoon aesthetic. I focus on creating engaging experiences and interactive works that are relatable and provocative. My aspiration in creating art is to create immersive experiences exploring its power in the conveyance of my Vietnamese culture, an expression of my personality. Growing up in Vietnam, I got to experience lots of memorable festivals and holidays during my childhood. In my current work, I combine photography and 3D modeling to recreate a traditional festival in my hometown as a way to relive and reflect upon the tradition, heritage, and shifting memories and experiences over time with child-like wonder.

ARTIST BIO

Currently working in the San Francisco Bay Area, Uyen Huynh is a digital media artist born and raised in Vietnam. She works across a range of digital media, including design, videography, photography, 3D modeling, and digital illustrations to create immersive and interactive experiences exploring Vietnamese culture and identity. She is also particularly interested in exploring the gaming field and creating interactive characters and experiences. Her work will be exhibited at the San Jose State University-Digital Media Arts Fall 2022 BFA Exhibition, Resonance virtually on New Art City.

Connections between this project and the big picture

My work focuses on expressing feelings through experience as well as creating virtual environment.
